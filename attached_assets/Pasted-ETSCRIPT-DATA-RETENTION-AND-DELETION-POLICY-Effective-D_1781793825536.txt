ETSCRIPT DATA RETENTION AND DELETION POLICY
Effective Date: June 18, 2026
Last Updated: June 18, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
This Data Retention and Deletion Policy explains how Etscript, a service operated by FurstoneTech Solutions Limited, retains, manages, archives, and deletes personal data and user content.

This Policy complements the Etscript Privacy Policy and forms part of our commitment to privacy, transparency, accountability, and responsible data management.

The purpose of this Policy is to ensure that personal data and user content are retained only for as long as necessary to:

Provide Etscript services;

Maintain platform security;

Fulfil contractual obligations;

Comply with legal, regulatory, accounting, and audit requirements; and

Protect the rights of users and FurstoneTech Solutions Limited.

2. GUIDING PRINCIPLES
Etscript applies the following principles when retaining and deleting information:

Data Minimization
We retain only the information necessary to operate the platform and fulfil legitimate business purposes.

Storage Limitation
Information shall not be retained indefinitely unless required by law or specifically requested by the user.

User Control
Users retain control over their manuscripts and may request deletion of their content and account data in accordance with this Policy.

Privacy by Design
Retention periods are designed to balance user convenience, platform functionality, security, and legal compliance.

4. RETENTION SCHEDULE
A. User Accounts
Active Accounts
Account information shall be retained for as long as the account remains active.

Inactive Accounts
Accounts with no activity for an extended period may be reviewed for archival or deletion after appropriate notice to the user.

No automatic deletion shall occur solely because a subscription expires.

B. Manuscripts and User Content
User Manuscripts
User manuscripts shall remain stored until:

Deleted by the user; or

Deleted through an approved account deletion process.

Subscription expiry shall not trigger manuscript deletion.

Users shall retain access to view stored manuscripts even when subscriptions have expired.

C. Generated Exports
Generated exports shall be retained for a maximum period of:

90 Days
After 90 days:

Exports may be automatically deleted;

Users may generate new exports where their plan permits.

This retention period helps manage storage costs while providing reasonable access to previously generated files.

D. Deleted Content
When a user deletes content:

Initial Deletion Stage
Content is marked for deletion and removed from normal user access.

Permanent Deletion Stage
Content shall be permanently deleted within:

30 Days
except where retention is required for:

Legal obligations;

Fraud investigations;

Security incidents;

Ongoing disputes.

E. Audit Logs
Audit and security logs shall generally be retained for:

12 Months
This includes:

Login activity

Security events

Authentication logs

Administrative actions

Retention supports security monitoring, incident response, and fraud prevention.

F. Payment and Financial Records
Payment-related records shall be retained for as long as required by:

Applicable tax laws;

Accounting obligations;

Audit requirements;

Fraud prevention obligations;

Other applicable legal or regulatory requirements.

Such records may be retained even after account deletion where legally necessary.

5. SUBSCRIPTION EXPIRY AND CONTENT RETENTION
Expiration of a subscription does not result in:

Account deletion;

Manuscript deletion;

Template deletion;

User profile deletion.

After subscription expiry, users may continue to:

Access their accounts;

View manuscripts;

Renew subscriptions;

Purchase one-time export services.

Users may not use premium formatting or export features until payment requirements are satisfied.

9. BACKUPS AND RECOVERY SYSTEMS
To ensure service reliability and business continuity, Etscript may maintain secure backup copies of certain information.

Backup data:

Is protected by appropriate security controls;

Is not routinely accessible to users;

Is retained only for disaster recovery and operational continuity purposes.

Where deleted information exists within backup systems, it shall be removed in accordance with backup rotation schedules and operational requirements.

10. USER RESPONSIBILITY
Users are encouraged to maintain their own copies of:

Manuscripts;

Research projects;

Books;

Business documents;

Generated exports.

While Etscript strives to maintain reliable systems, users remain responsible for preserving copies of important content.

11. INTERNATIONAL DATA STORAGE
Certain service providers used by Etscript may store or process information outside Nigeria.

Where international processing occurs, Etscript relies on service providers that implement technical, contractual, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data.

12. POLICY ENFORCEMENT
FurstoneTech Solutions Limited shall periodically review retention practices to ensure compliance with:

Applicable data protection laws;

Security requirements;

Business needs;

Platform operations.

Retention periods may be updated where necessary to reflect legal or operational changes.
