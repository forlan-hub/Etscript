# 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

## Vision

To create the easiest manuscript formatting and publishing preparation platform for writers, authors, consultants, trainers, academics, publishers, and organizations.

The platform shall allow users to upload or paste manuscripts, choose a writing format, publishing target, and design theme, then automatically generate professionally formatted publication-ready documents.

The platform SHALL NOT rewrite, summarize, improve grammar, or alter the author's intellectual property.

The platform's sole purpose is professional formatting and publishing preparation.

# 11. FORMATTING ENGINE

Purpose

Transform manuscript structure according to selected template.

The engine SHALL NOT:

* Rewrite text
* Correct grammar
* Correct spelling
* Summarize content

---

The engine SHALL:

* Detect chapters
* Apply typography rules
* Apply spacing rules
* Apply page numbering
* Apply margins
* Apply headers and footers
* Generate front matter
* Generate back matter

based on user selections.
