1. PRODUCT VISION
Etscript is a web-based manuscript and document formatting platform that transforms raw user content into professionally formatted, publication-ready documents through structured formatting templates.
The platform is designed to eliminate the technical complexity of document formatting and enable users to focus on content creation while Etscript handles formatting, styling, pagination, document structure, and export preparation.
Etscript is not an AI writing platform and shall not generate content on behalf of users. Its primary function is professional formatting, document organization, citation management, and export generation.

3. CORE PRODUCT PRINCIPLE
Content and Formatting Separation
User content shall remain separate from formatting rules.
The system shall never permanently alter original manuscript content.
Formatting instructions shall be applied through template layers and rendering engines.
Benefits:
•	Template switching
•	Formatting previews
•	Undo capability
•	Multiple export styles from one manuscript
